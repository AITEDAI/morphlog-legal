FaceLog Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 7, 2026

FaceLog ("the App") values your privacy. This policy describes how we handle your data.

1. Camera & ARKit (TrueDepth)

The App uses the camera and ARKit TrueDepth sensor to record daily facial changes.

  • Data collected: facial photos, 3D mesh (vertex coordinates), blend shape coefficients
  • All data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.
  • 3D mesh and blend shape data are used solely for facial condition analysis.

2. HealthKit (Health Data)

The App reads HealthKit data to analyze correlations between facial condition and lifestyle.

  • Read items: sleep duration/efficiency, step count, resting heart rate, heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Read-only access — the App does not write data to HealthKit.
  • Data read from HealthKit is stored and analyzed only on your device.

3. Location

  • The App requests location access to retrieve local weather data (temperature, humidity).
  • GPS coordinates are not stored. Only temperature and humidity values are saved on your device.

4. Subscription Management (RevenueCat)

  • The App uses the RevenueCat SDK for subscription payment processing.
  • RevenueCat performs Apple ID-based receipt validation only and does not collect personally identifiable information.
  • RevenueCat Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

5. User Notes

  • Optional notes entered by the user are stored only on the device.

6. Analytics Data

  • All analysis including Face Score and insights is processed 100% on-device.
  • Analysis results are never transmitted to external servers.

7. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect the Advertising Identifier (IDFA).
  • We do not use third-party tracking tools.
  • We do not transmit user data to external servers.

8. Data Deletion

Deleting the App removes all data stored on your device, including photos, analysis results, notes, and health data copies. You can also delete all data from within the App's settings.

9. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Future Changes

We may introduce the following features to improve the service:

  • Crashlytics (app stability monitoring)
  • Firebase Analytics (usage statistics)
  • CloudKit (iCloud backup/sync)
  • AI Coaching (server-based analysis)

Any changes will be reflected in an updated version of this policy, and users will be notified within the App.
